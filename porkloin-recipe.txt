Ingredients:
 - 2 lb boneless porkloin roast
 - 2 tablespoons steak seasoning rub
 - 1/2 cup balasamic vinegar
 - 1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:
 1. Dissolve steak seasoning in balsamic vinegar, then stir in olive oil. Place
    pork into resealable plastic bag and pour marinade overtop. Squeeze out 
    air and seal bag; marinate 2 hours to overnight.
 
 2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
 
 3. Place pork into a glass baking dish along with marinade. Bake in preheated 
    oven, basting occasionally until the pork reaches an internal temperature 
    of 145 degrees F (65 degrees C), about 1 hour. Let the roast rest for 10 
    minutes before slicing and serving.

Pairs well with:
  red potatos w/herbs
  grilled asparagus
